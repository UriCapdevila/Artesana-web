# **App Name**: Artesana: Conecta con la esencia.

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display high-quality images and descriptions of the handcrafted books, notebooks, and other creations.
- Call to Action Button: Prominent WhatsApp button for direct inquiries and purchases, redirecting users to the emprendimiento's WhatsApp chat.
- Instagram Feed Integration: Embed an Instagram feed to showcase the latest products and behind-the-scenes content.
- Customer Testimonials: Display customer testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- AI-Powered Product Description Tool: An AI tool that automatically generates unique and engaging product descriptions for each item, highlighting their artisanal qualities and unique features.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy terracotta (#E07A5F) to evoke a sense of craftsmanship and warmth.
- Background color: Light beige (#F8F0E3), a very desaturated tint of terracotta, for a soft and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#3D405B) to add a touch of sophistication and contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif typeface for both headlines and body text, to give a elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel.
- Hand-drawn style icons to reinforce the artisanal theme.
- Clean and spacious layout to showcase products without distractions.
- Subtle page transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience.
